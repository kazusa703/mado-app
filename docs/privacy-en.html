Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

MADO ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

The App stores training performance data (thresholds, reaction times, accuracy, etc.) locally on your device only. This data is not transmitted to any external server.

2. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) for ad delivery. You will be asked for permission to use the advertising identifier when you first use the App.

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. In-App Purchases

The App offers in-app purchases through Apple's StoreKit. Payment information is managed by Apple and is not accessible to the developer.

4. Third-Party Sharing

Except for the advertising services described above, the App does not sell, share, or provide your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete your training data from Settings > Delete All Data within the App. Uninstalling the App will also delete all data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated without prior notice. Significant changes will be communicated through the App.

8. Contact

For privacy inquiries: imaiissatsu@gmail.com
