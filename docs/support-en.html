FAQ

Q. How does the training work?

You identify shapes displayed at the center of the screen. The difficulty adjusts automatically based on your responses, measuring your attention threshold.

Q. How many sessions can I play per day?

The free version allows 1 session per day. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited sessions.

Q. How do I restore my purchase?

Go to the Settings tab and tap "Restore Purchase".

Q. How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings tab > "Delete All Data". This action cannot be undone.

Q. Can this be used for medical purposes?

This App is not a medical device. It is not intended for the diagnosis, treatment, or prevention of dementia. If you have health concerns, please consult a medical professional.
